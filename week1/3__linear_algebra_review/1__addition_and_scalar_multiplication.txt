Matrix addition:
    needs to have the same dimension
    A =
        |0 1|
        |3 4|
        |6 7|
        |2 5|
    B =
        |0 1|
        |1 0|
        |0 1|
        |1 0|
    A + B =
        |0 2|
        |4 4|
        |6 8|
        |3 5|

Scalar multiplication/division:
    2*B =
        |0 2|
        |2 0|
        |0 2|
        |2 0|
