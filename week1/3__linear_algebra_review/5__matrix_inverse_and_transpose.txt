# vim: ft=indentcolor

Inverse:
    - A needs to be MxM
    - if A has an inverse:
        + A * A-1 = A-1 * A = I
    - e.g.:
        |3  4| * |0.4   -0.1 |  = I
        |2 16| * |-0.05 0.075|

Transpose:
    - A =
        |1 2 3|
        |4 5 6|
    - AT =
        |1 4|
        |2 5|
        |3 6|
